Career Goals

My long-term goal is to design and develop embedded systems that bridge the gap between hardware and intelligent software — especially in robotics, IoT, or smart energy applications. I want to contribute to systems that are efficient, connected, and sustainable.

My Roadmap

  1. Gain hands-on experience through co-ops or internships in embedded systems.
  2. Master C/C++, real-time operating systems, and microcontroller design.
  3. Contribute to open-source hardware and IoT frameworks.
  4. Earn advanced certifications in embedded design or networking.
  5. Grow into a systems engineer or R&D role focusing on intelligent hardware.
